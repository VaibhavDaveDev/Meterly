# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Landlords and tenants sharing properties with solar panels in India. A single user can act as an owner for one property and a tenant for another using the same account.

## Product Purpose
To automate the calculation of monthly electricity bills for properties with solar panels. It ensures absolute transparency by showing exactly how the calculation is derived from solar and grid meters.

## Brand Personality
Transparent, direct, honest. The UI should feel calm, professional, and utilitarian, drawing inspiration from Intercom's design system (cream canvas, charcoal type, minimal chrome).

## Anti-references
SaaS clichés (gradient text, glowing buttons), nested cards, heavy borders, vague or generic "AI slop" aesthetics, and over-engineered dashboard layouts.

## Design Principles
- **Absolute Transparency:** Every number on every bill shows exactly where it came from. No black boxes.
- **Form follows Data:** The UI must be driven by the inherent structure and meaning of the data. 
- **Inverted Pyramid:** Lead with high-level summaries (KPIs) and provide progressive disclosure to granular details.
- **Invisible UI:** Crucial functionality (tooltips, secondary actions) should be revealed contextually rather than cluttering the default state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear typography (Inter for body, Geist for headings, Geist Mono for numbers).
- High contrast and readability, avoiding purely decorative visual elements.
- Accessible by default, ensuring all users can clearly understand the billing calculations.
